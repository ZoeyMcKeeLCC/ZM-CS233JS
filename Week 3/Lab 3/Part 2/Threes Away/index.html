Triple Trouble!

Roll the dice to get the lowest score possible!

Game Setup

How to Play:

  • Roll all non-held in dice every round.
  • All dice are worth their face value except 3's are worth 0.
  • Once a dice has been held it cannot be unheld.
  • Having 3 die with the same value adds 10 to your score.
  • You must hold at least one more die per turn
  • The player with the lowest score wins!
